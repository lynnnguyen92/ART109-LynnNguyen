Game Description

Even though you're not an enthusiast of jigsaw, you must have played at least a game or two in tender age. Now you can recall the pleasure of the traditional pastime with our randomly generated puzzles! In the game, you will be given an image cut into small pieces, and you can click the pieces to move and assemble them so as to reconstruct the original picture.
