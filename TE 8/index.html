Technical Exercise 8: Paper.js

Meta Balls Example

For this exercise, I have modified some of the elements:

- Changing the color of the path from black to gradient colors
- Twisting the position of how the path is initialized
- Adding music when the mouse is pressed
- Changing the number of points and the speed of the path when moving the mouse on the canvas

In general, I want to change the example from a simple wave movement of the paths to something that looks like a sound wave. I want to emulate the experience of watching the sunset while listening to music.
